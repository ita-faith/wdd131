Site Plan: Green Brew Café

Site Name

Green Brew Café - An eco-friendly café in Lagos offering organic coffee, tea, and sustainable food options.

Site Purpose

The website provides information about Green Brew Café, including its menu, location, sustainability efforts, and contact details.

Scenarios

  • What are the café’s opening hours?
  • Does the café offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Color Scheme

  • Primary: #132A13 (Headings, borders)
  • Secondary: #31572C (Subheadings, highlights)
  • Accent1: #4F772D (Buttons, links)
  • Accent2: #90A955 (Backgrounds, hover effects)
  • Light Background: #ECF39E (Main background color)

Typography

  • Headings: Italics, weight 100-700, black
  • Body Text: Arima, weight 100-700
